  • Who am I

I'm a student in Universidad Rey Juan Carlos, studying the last year of Design and Development of Video Games

Part of Virtual Soul (and association inside the university which main focus is teaching videogame development using people from inside the industry to give the talks) and developer of two games in Game Jams (competitions where teams try to make a complete video game from scrath). Developer of Camlann, La Mazmorra maldita de Materac and Hellscape: Threechery

Main gameplay designer and mechanic's programmer of Camlann

A project created for college. In this multiplayer fast-paced game two duelist face each other until one of them reachs the end of the map.

  • Game's page

Main gameplay designer, balance director and support programmer of La Mazmorra Maldita de Materac

A project created for college. In this Tactical RPG, you'll have to guide a party of 4 adventurers through the Cursed Dungeon of Materac, in which your weapons have become useless. You'll have to use your wits to go through the trials that awaits you.

  • Game's Page

Main gameplay and level designer of Hellscape: Threechery

A project created for the Gamegen Game Jam - Segunda edición. In this game, three different persons will have to work together to get out of hell itself, completing a series of puzzles in which they'll have to work together.

  • Game's Page

Main designer and programmer of Disco Inferno

A project created for the Gamegen Game Jam - Tercera edición. In this game, the player has to listen to the sound that comes out of four different portals and guess which one is the correct one. Their only clue is that the music that comes from the fake portals is incomplete.
